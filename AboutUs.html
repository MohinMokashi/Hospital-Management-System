About Us

MedWey Healthcare Limited – an IHH Healthcare Berhad Company – is a leading integrated healthcare services provider in India. It is one of the largest healthcare organizations in the country with 28 healthcare facilities, 4,500+ operational beds (including O&M facilities), and over 400 diagnostics centers (including JVs). MedWay is present in India, the United Arab Emirates (UAE), Nepal & Sri Lanka. The Company is listed on the BSE Ltd and National Stock Exchange (NSE) of India. It draws strength

Mission

To enhance the quality of healthcare delivery by providing an integrated, user-friendly management system that streamlines operations, improves patient care, and fosters collaboration among healthcare providers, ensuring that every patient receives timely and effective treatment.

Vision

To be a leading healthcare management solution that empowers healthcare organizations to achieve excellence in patient care through innovative technology, data-driven insights, and a commitment to continuous improvement, ultimately contributing to healthier communities worldwide.
